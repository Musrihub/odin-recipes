Description

Pumpkins are often yellowish to orange in colour, and they vary from oblate to globular to oblong; some feature a white rind. The rind is smooth and usually lightly furrowed or ribbed. The fruit stem is hard and woody, ridged, and angled.

Ingredients

  • vitamins A,B1, B6 and C
  • copper
  • fiber
  • folate
  • manganese

Steps

  1. Cut into the pumpkin. Place your pumpkin on a solid surface you can wash (or spread newspaper to catch any drips)
  2. Pull out the guts and seeds.
  3. Draw your design.
  4. Cut out your design.
  5. Condition your jack-o'-lantern.
  6. Illuminate your jack-o'-lantern.
